Hick's Law

Wikipedia

www.wikipedia.org

Wikipedia is available in over 300 languages, however the intro page shows the links to only 10 of the most available languages so as to ensure that the user is not immeaditely overwhelmed with what would otherwise be too many choices.

Visual Hierarchy

National Security Agency

www.nsa.gov

The main page of the NSA website practices good visual hierarchy by using different fonts and colors to immeaditely catch the attention of the user to the most important headlines, such as what their mission is and how they accomplish that.
