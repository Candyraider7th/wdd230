Site Name

Los Alamos Chamber of Commerce

Site Purpose

As stated by Indeed.com, "A chamber of commerce is typically a local organization of businesses that work together to strengthen the business interests of the group." For the Los Alamos Chamber of Commerce, a critical priority will be to assist small business owners in a large town that has relatively easy access to major chains such as Walmart, JcPenny, etc. Thus, the purpose of the website will be to focus on attracting potential and new business owners, and providing assisstance to subscribers.
